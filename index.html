About Me

I am currently an astrophysics PhD student at Michigan State University, working with Laura Chomiuk. My research interests include studying the ejecta of classical novae with the Very Large Array, constraining the Galactic nova rate with the All-Sky Automated Survey for Supernovae, and timing pulsars with single dish radio telescopes.
